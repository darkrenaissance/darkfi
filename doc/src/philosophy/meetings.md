# Discussion group

Every other Wednesday at 15:00 UTC/16:00 CET/10:00 EST, there is a meeting on
[darkirc](../misc/darkirc/darkirc.md) in `#philosophy`.  Come and discuss different
philosophy texts with fellow community members.
